Donation Instructions

Donations can support meals, school items, personal hygiene, clothing, activities, and maintenance needs. Please confirm current needs before sending items.

Suggested Items

  • Rice, noodles, canned food, cooking oil, sugar, and milk powder.
  • Soap, shampoo, toothpaste, toothbrushes, towels, and cleaning supplies.
  • School bags, exercise books, pens, pencils, uniforms, and learning materials.
  • Blankets, bedsheets, clothes, shoes, and sports equipment in good condition.

Before Donating

Important: This student site is not an official donation channel. Contact the organization directly before making any payment or delivery.

Search for official donation information

Responsible Giving Steps

1. Verify

Use official contact details or trusted public information to confirm the home's current needs.

2. Prepare

Pack donated items neatly, check expiry dates, and avoid damaged or unsuitable goods.

3. Deliver

Arrange a suitable time for delivery and keep communication respectful and clear.
